About Craft By Anjali

Our Story of Passion and Creativity

Our Journey

Craft By Anjali was born from a deep passion for traditional Indian art forms and a desire to preserve the rich cultural heritage of rangoli making. What started as a personal hobby has blossomed into a beautiful celebration of handcrafted rangoli patches.

Each patch is created with meticulous attention to detail, combining traditional patterns with contemporary aesthetics to appeal to art lovers everywhere.

🎨
✨

Our Mission

We believe that art should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to bring the beauty of traditional rangoli designs into modern homes through high-quality, affordable patches.

Every patch we create tells a story of dedication, creativity, and love for our craft. We take pride in providing not just products, but pieces of art that bring joy and color to your spaces.
