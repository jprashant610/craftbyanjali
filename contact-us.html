Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Visit our Items page, add patches to your cart, and click "Place Order on WhatsApp" to complete your order directly through WhatsApp.

What are the dimensions of the patches?

Each patch comes with detailed dimensions listed on its product page. Most patches range from 2" to 12" depending on the design.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. Contact us via WhatsApp for international shipping inquiries and rates.

Can I customize a design?

Absolutely! We offer custom orders. Message us on WhatsApp to discuss your specific requirements.

What's the typical delivery time?

Regular orders are shipped within 5-7 business days. Custom orders may take 10-14 days.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments through WhatsApp. You can pay via bank transfer, UPI, or other methods as discussed during checkout.
